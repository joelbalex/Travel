Thanks For Looking!


I'm David Leiter, the guy behind this website. I'm an American who's been traveling the world full time for 8 years now.

I started this travel blog in 2019 to document my own international trips, share my photos, and help others learn how to travel the world and find some really good spots off the beaten path.

I'm currently based in Bali, Indonesia, where I met my wife Intan, who’s a Bali local. Now she joins me on these wild and crazy adventures too.

CULTURAL AND HISTORICAL DESTINATIONS

Cultural and Historical Destinations: A Journey Through Time Exploring cultural and historical destinations offers a captivating journey through time, where travelers can immerse themselves in the rich heritage of iconic civilizations. From the ancient ruins of Machu Picchu to the majestic Pyramids of Egypt, these destinations provide a deep connection to the past. Travel packages often include guided tours of renowned landmarks like Rome's Colosseum, China's Great Wall, and Greece's Acropolis, offering insights into the art, architecture, and traditions that shaped our world. These experiences not only educate but also create unforgettable memories, making them a perfect addition to any travel itinerary.

NATURAL DESTINATIONS

Discover the Beauty of Natural Destinations Natural destinations offer travelers a chance to escape into the breathtaking beauty of the world's most awe-inspiring landscapes. Whether it's the pristine beaches of the Maldives, the towering peaks of the Swiss Alps, or the lush rainforests of Costa Rica, these destinations are a haven for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike. Travel packages often include curated experiences such as hiking through New Zealand’s Fiordland National Park, exploring the vibrant coral reefs of Australia's Great Barrier Reef, or witnessing the majestic waterfalls of Iceland. These packages typically combine outdoor activities like kayaking, wildlife safaris, and guided nature walks with luxurious accommodations that place you right in the heart of these stunning environments. Perfect for anyone looking to reconnect with nature, these journeys offer not just relaxation but a deep appreciation for the planet's most remarkable natural wonders.

ADVENTURE DESTINATIONS

Thrill-Seeker’s Guide to Adventure Destinations For those who crave adrenaline-pumping experiences, adventure destinations offer the ultimate escape into the wild. From the rugged terrains of Patagonia to the challenging peaks of Mount Kilimanjaro, these destinations are tailor-made for thrill-seekers. Travel packages often include everything from trekking the Inca Trail to Machu Picchu, white-water rafting in the Grand Canyon, to diving with sharks in South Africa. These packages are designed to maximize excitement while ensuring safety, with expert guides leading the way through each exhilarating experience. Whether it's zip-lining through Costa Rican rainforests, skiing down the slopes of the French Alps, or sandboarding in the Sahara Desert, adventure travel offers unforgettable moments that push the limits and create lifelong memories. Perfect for those looking to challenge themselves and explore the world’s most extreme environments, these trips are an invitation to step out of your comfort zone and embrace the extraordinary.

RELAXATION AND WELLNESS DESTINATIONS

Rejuvenate in Relaxation and Wellness Destinations Relaxation and wellness destinations are perfect for those looking to unwind, rejuvenate, and reconnect with themselves in serene surroundings. From the tranquil beaches of Bali to the thermal baths of Iceland, these destinations offer a peaceful escape from the stresses of everyday life. Travel packages often include stays at luxurious spa resorts, yoga retreats in the Himalayas, or wellness sanctuaries in Thailand, where guests can indulge in holistic treatments, meditation sessions, and organic cuisine. These packages are designed to cater to both the body and mind, offering a blend of relaxation, wellness activities, and cultural experiences. Whether it’s soaking in Japan’s hot springs, enjoying Ayurvedic treatments in India, or simply lounging by a turquoise lagoon in the Maldives, these trips provide the perfect environment for complete relaxation. Ideal for anyone seeking a restorative getaway, these destinations promise to leave you feeling refreshed, balanced, and ready to take on the world.

RELIGIOUS AND SPIRITUAL DESTINATIONS

Journey to Religious and Spiritual Destinations Religious and spiritual destinations offer profound experiences that go beyond mere travel, inviting visitors to explore sacred sites and embark on personal journeys of faith and reflection. From the holy city of Jerusalem to the sacred banks of the Ganges in Varanasi, these destinations are steeped in spiritual significance. Travel packages often include pilgrimages to revered sites like the Vatican in Rome, the temples of Kyoto, or the monasteries of Tibet, providing guided tours that delve into the history, rituals, and beliefs that define these sacred places. These packages are thoughtfully crafted to allow for both structured exploration and personal reflection, with opportunities for meditation, prayer, and participation in local religious ceremonies. Whether it's walking the Camino de Santiago in Spain, attending a spiritual retreat in India, or visiting the ancient temples of Angkor Wat in Cambodia, these journeys offer a deep connection to the spiritual heritage of humanity. Perfect for those seeking meaning, peace, or enlightenment, these trips promise transformative experiences in some of the world’s most revered spiritual landscapes.

FESTIVALS AND EVENTS

Celebrate at Festivals and Events Destinations Festivals and events destinations offer a vibrant and immersive way to experience the world’s diverse cultures through their most lively celebrations. From the electrifying Carnival in Rio de Janeiro to the colorful Diwali festival in India, these destinations are alive with music, dance, and tradition. Travel packages to these events often include guided experiences that take you to the heart of the festivities, offering front-row access to iconic celebrations like Oktoberfest in Germany, the Lantern Festival in Taiwan, or the Cherry Blossom Festival in Japan. These packages typically bundle festival tickets, accommodations, and cultural tours, ensuring you don’t miss any of the excitement. Whether it’s dancing in the streets of New Orleans during Mardi Gras, witnessing the running of the bulls in Pamplona, or enjoying world-class performances at the Edinburgh Festival, these trips provide unforgettable experiences that capture the essence of each destination’s unique culture. Perfect for travelers who love to celebrate and connect with people from around the world, these festival journeys promise to be as exhilarating as they are enriching.

EXOTIC AND REMOTE DESTINATIONS

Escape to Exotic and Remote Destinations For those seeking unparalleled adventure and seclusion, exotic and remote destinations offer a journey into the world’s most untouched and extraordinary places. From the isolated islands of the Seychelles to the mysterious landscapes of Antarctica, these destinations promise a once-in-a-lifetime experience far from the beaten path. Travel packages to these remote locations often include all-inclusive itineraries, featuring private yacht charters to the Galápagos Islands, guided expeditions through the jungles of Borneo, or luxury stays in overwater bungalows in Bora Bora. These packages are designed to blend adventure with comfort, offering exclusive access to pristine environments and rare wildlife encounters, all while ensuring a seamless and luxurious experience. Whether it’s exploring the sand dunes of Namibia, trekking through Patagonia’s wild terrain, or diving in the crystal-clear waters of Fiji, these journeys provide a rare opportunity to discover the world’s most exotic and remote corners. Ideal for the intrepid traveler seeking solitude, natural beauty, and unique cultures, these destinations offer an unforgettable escape into the extraordinary.

LUXURY DESTINATIONS

Indulge in Luxury Destinations For those who seek the pinnacle of comfort and elegance, luxury destinations offer a world of opulence and exclusivity. From the stunning private villas in the Maldives to the sophisticated city escapes in Paris, these destinations provide the ultimate in indulgence. Travel packages to luxury destinations often include stays at five-star resorts, private tours of iconic landmarks, and personalized experiences such as private yacht charters in the French Riviera or bespoke shopping tours in Milan. These packages are crafted to deliver unparalleled service and lavish amenities, including gourmet dining, spa treatments, and VIP access to exclusive events. Whether you’re savoring a sunset cruise in the Caribbean, relaxing in a luxurious overwater bungalow in Bora Bora, or enjoying a royal treatment at a palace hotel in Dubai, these trips offer a blend of world-class luxury and exceptional experiences. Perfect for those who desire the finest in travel, these luxury destinations promise not only comfort and style but also unforgettable moments in some of the world’s most glamorous settings.
